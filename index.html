Hao Zheng

Ph.D. Student, Computer Science and Engineering

My research focuses on the intersection of medical image analysis and artificial intelligence. I am dedicated to designing data-efficient learning methods for 2D/3D biomedical image segmentation and improving model generalization on multi-domain/site data. My studies are driven by real applications in Medical Imaging and related fields. I also have expertise in deep learning for high-dimensional scientific data visulization.
