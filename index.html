Hao Zheng 郑昊


I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Center for Biomedical Image Computing and Analytics (CBICA) of University of Pennsylvania. I obtained my Ph.D. from University of Notre Dame in 2021, and my B.Sc. from University of Science and Technology of China in 2016. My research focuses on the intersection of medical image analysis and artificial intelligence, especially in designing data-efficient learning methods for 2D/3D biomedical image segmentation and improving model generalization on multi-domain/site data.
